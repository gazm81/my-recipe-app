Chuck Steak Chilli Con Carne

Serves6–8
Prep Time20 mins
Cook TimePressure: 40 mins + Simmer: 15-20 mins
Sourceoriginal

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 kg chuck steak, trimmed and cut into 2–3 cm cubes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (or beef dripping if you've got it)
  • 2 onions, finely diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 red capsicums, diced
  • 2–3 fresh chillies, chopped (optional, adjust heat level)
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1–2 tsp chilli powder (to taste)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 × 400 g tins chopped tomatoes
  • 2 × 400 g tins beans (kidney, black, pinto — mix is nice), drained
  • 500 ml beef stock (or half stock / half dark beer)
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 small square dark chocolate (70%) (or 1 tsp cocoa powder)
  • 1–2 tsp brown sugar (to balance acidity)
  • Salt & black pepper
  • 1 stick celery + 1 carrot, finely diced (optional - adds sweetness/body)
  • 1 tbsp chipotle in adobo or ½ tsp chipotle powder (optional - smoky heat)
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)

👨‍🍳 Method

  1. 🔥 Brown the beef
  2. Heat oil in your pressure cooker/large pot.
  3. Sear chuck steak in small batches until well browned. Remove and set aside.
  4. 🧅 Build the base
  5. Add onion, capsicum, celery/carrot (if using). Sauté until softened.
  6. Stir in garlic, fresh chilli, and all dried spices. Cook 1–2 min until fragrant.
  7. Add tomato paste and cook another minute (this deepens flavour).
  8. 🥘 Combine
  9. Return beef to the pot.
  10. Add tomatoes, beef stock/beer, Worcestershire, bay leaf (if using).
  11. Stir, bring to a simmer.
  12. ⏲️ Cook
  13. Pressure cooker: 40 min on High Pressure, natural release.
  14. Slow cooker: 8 hrs on Low or 5 hrs on High.
  15. Stovetop/oven: 2.5–3 hrs gentle simmer, lid on, stir occasionally.
  16. ✨ Finish
  17. Once beef is fork-tender, stir in beans, chocolate, and sugar.
  18. Simmer gently for 15–20 min (uncovered) to thicken and let flavours meld.
  19. Taste, adjust salt/pepper, balance with more sugar/chilli if needed.

⏰ Timing

Total Time: ~1.5 hours (20 min prep + 40 min pressure + 15-20 min finishing) | Method: Pressure Cook + Simmer | Serving: With rice, tortillas, or cornbread. Top with sour cream, cheese, jalapeños, and fresh coriander. Leftovers improve overnight!

🥂 Recommended Drinks

  • Mexican beer
  • Dark beer or stout
  • Red wine
  • Sparkling water with lime
