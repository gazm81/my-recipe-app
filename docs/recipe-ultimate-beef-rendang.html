Ultimate Instant Pot Beef Rendang

Serves4
Prep Time15 mins
Cook Time~2.5 hours
Sourcetraditional

🛒 Ingredients

  • 800g beef chuck, cut into 4–5 cm chunks
  • 1 jar Rendang curry paste (180–200g)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced (or 1 tsp jarred garlic)
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced (or jar)
  • 400ml coconut cream (not milk)
  • 1/2 cup beef stock
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 1 dried bay leaf (or 2 kaffir lime leaves)
  • 1/2 cup toasted shredded coconut (optional but recommended)
  • Oil for sautéing
  • Salt and sugar, to taste

👨‍🍳 Method

  1. Set Instant Pot to Sauté mode.
  2. Add oil and sauté onion until soft (2–3 minutes).
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and the Rendang paste. Cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Push mixture to one side. Add beef chunks in batches, searing both sides (2–3 mins total per batch).
  5. Stir everything together to coat the beef well.
  6. Add coconut cream, beef stock, cinnamon stick, and bay leaf.
  7. Stir and scrape up browned bits.
  8. Pressure cook on High Pressure for 20 minutes.
  9. Perform Quick Release.
  10. Switch to Slow Cook mode, low heat, lid on but vented or glass lid preferred.
  11. Let simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  12. Switch back to Sauté mode, lid off.
  13. Stir in toasted coconut (if using).
  14. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring every few minutes, until sauce is thick, dark, and clinging to the beef.
  15. Add salt and sugar to taste (start with ½ tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar).

⏰ Timing

🔥 Step 1: Build the base (Sauté – 10 minutes) | ⚙️ Step 2: Tenderize (Pressure Cook – 20 minutes) | 💤 Step 3: Slow develop flavor (Slow Cook – 1 hour) | 🔥 Step 4: Final reduction (Sauté – 15 minutes) | 🍚 Serve with steamed jasmine or basmati rice, garnish with chopped coriander and lime wedge

🥂 Recommended Drinks

  • Indonesian beer
  • Coconut water
  • Jasmine tea
  • Sparkling water with lime
