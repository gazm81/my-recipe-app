Mums Self-Saucing Chocolate Pudding

Serves6-8
Prep Time15 mins
Cook Time30-35 mins
Sourcefamily

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 cup (150g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (25g) cocoa powder
  • 2/3 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup (125ml) milk
  • 2 tbsp (30g) unsalted butter, melted (or neutral oil)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (100g) brown sugar (for sauce)
  • 2 tbsp (20g) cocoa powder (for sauce)
  • 1 1/4 cups (310ml) boiling water (for sauce)

👨‍🍳 Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F)
  2. Grease a 1.5L (6-cup) baking dish with butter or cooking spray
  3. In the bowl of your Breville stand mixer, add the flour, cocoa powder, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt
  4. Using the flat beater on the 'Light Mixing' setting, stir until combined
  5. Add milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract, then mix on the same setting until smooth
  6. Pour the batter into the greased baking dish and spread evenly with a spatula
  7. Combine the brown sugar and cocoa powder in a small bowl
  8. Sprinkle this dry mixture evenly over the batter in the dish
  9. Slowly pour the boiling water over the back of a spoon to cover the top (this ensures it doesn't disrupt the batter)
  10. Place the dish in the oven and bake for 30–35 minutes or until the top is firm to the touch, and a luscious sauce is bubbling underneath
  11. Allow the pudding to cool for about 5 minutes
  12. Scoop into bowls, ensuring you get both the cake and the sauce
  13. Serve with vanilla ice cream, whipped cream, or a dollop of custard

⏰ Timing

Total Time: 50 minutes (15 min prep + 30-35 min baking + 5 min cooling)
