Pork Carnitas (2 kg) – Pressure Cooker + Air Fryer

Serves8–10
Prep Time10 mins
Cook Time70 min pressure + 6-8 min air fry
Sourceoriginal

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 kg pork shoulder, trimmed of excess fat and cut into 4–5 large chunks
  • 30 g taco seasoning (about 2 heaped tablespoons)
  • 200 g frozen diced onion
  • 15–20 g jarred minced garlic (1 heaped tablespoon = ~15 g)
  • 250 ml total liquid (choose one option below):
  • Option 1: 250 ml chicken stock
  • Option 2: 125 ml stock + 125 ml orange juice
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: extra taco seasoning or smoked paprika to finish

👨‍🍳 Method

  1. Rub pork pieces all over with 30 g taco seasoning.
  2. Lightly season with salt and pepper if your taco mix is low-salt.
  3. Add frozen onion and jarred garlic to the bottom of your pressure cooker.
  4. Pour in liquid (stock/juice mix) and lime juice.
  5. Place pork pieces on top.
  6. Seal lid and cook on high pressure for 70 minutes.
  7. Use quick release when done.
  8. Remove pork and shred with two forks.
  9. Discard excess fat if visible. You can mix the onions and juices back in or reduce the liquid separately for extra richness.
  10. Preheat air fryer to 200°C (400°F).
  11. Place shredded pork in a single layer (work in batches).
  12. Air fry for 6–8 minutes, shaking/tossing halfway through until edges are crispy.
  13. Serve with warm tortillas, avocado, salsa, sour cream, cheese, lime wedges, or on rice, in burrito bowls, or with corn chips.

⏰ Timing

Total Time: ~1 hr 25 min (10 min prep + 70 min pressure cook + 6-8 min air fry per batch)

🥂 Recommended Drinks

  • Mexican beer
  • Margaritas
  • Lime sparkling water
  • Horchata
