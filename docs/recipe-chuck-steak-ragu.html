Chuck Steak Ragu (Hybrid Method – 1kg)

Serves4
Prep Time20 mins
Cook TimePressure: 25 mins + Slow: 2 hours
Sourceoriginal

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 kg chuck steak, cut into 5–6 cm chunks
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 50 g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 stick celery, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1½ tbsp tomato paste
  • 125 ml (½ cup) red wine
  • 400 g (1 can) crushed tomatoes
  • 250 ml beef stock
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 sprig rosemary or thyme
  • 100 ml full cream milk or cream
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • ~400 g pasta (pappardelle), to serve
  • Parmesan, to serve

👨‍🍳 Method

  1. ⚡ Step 1: Sauté & Build Base
  2. Set Instant Pot to Sauté (Normal).
  3. Heat olive oil. Brown chuck steak in batches (~5 mins each side). Remove and set aside.
  4. Add pancetta, cook 2 mins until it starts to render.
  5. Add onion, carrot, and celery. Sauté 6–8 mins until softened.
  6. Stir in garlic and tomato paste. Cook for 1 minute.
  7. Pour in red wine to deglaze. Scrape up browned bits. Simmer 2–3 mins.
  8. Add beef, tomatoes, stock, bay leaf, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  9. ⚡ Step 2: Pressure Cook
  10. Cancel Sauté. Lock the lid. Set: Pressure Cook (High) → 25 minutes
  11. Let it Natural Release for 10 minutes, then Quick Release remaining pressure.
  12. 🔸 Optional Freeze Step: Allow to cool slightly and transfer to a labelled 22×25 cm freezer bag. Label: 'Chuck Ragu – Pressure phase only. To finish: Slow cook LOW 2 hrs + 100 ml milk/cream.'
  13. 🐢 Step 3: Slow Cook
  14. Shred beef gently with forks in the pot.
  15. Switch to Slow Cook → LOW → 2 hours (Lid slightly ajar for reduction. Stir occasionally.)
  16. In final 15–30 mins, stir in milk or cream.
  17. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  18. 🍽️ Step 4: Serve
  19. Cook pasta separately. Toss with ragu or spoon over.
  20. Top with Parmesan and fresh herbs if desired.

⏰ Timing

Total Time: ~3 hours (20 min prep + 25 min pressure + 10 min release + 2 hr slow cook) | Method: Pressure Cook + Slow Cook | Appliance: Instant Pot 9-in-1 Duo Plus (5.7L) | Freezer-friendly: Yes (after pressure phase)

🥂 Recommended Drinks

  • Italian red wine (Chianti or Sangiovese)
  • Full-bodied red wine
  • Italian beer
  • Sparkling water with lemon
