Mum's Lasagne

Serves4
Prep Time45 mins
Cook Time35-40 mins
Sourcefamily

ğŸ›’ Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped (frozen will do 250gm)
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped (minced garlic will do)
  • 500g beef mince
  • 5 tbsp tomato paste
  • 800g canned tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Dash of red wine
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp Italian herbs
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • 125g mozzarella or cheddar cheese
  • 60g Parmesan or cheddar cheese, grated
  • 250g San Remo Lasagne Sheets

ğŸ‘¨â€ğŸ³ Method

  1. Heat oil in large pan
  2. Add onion, carrot and garlic. Cook until soft
  3. Add beef mince, cook until brown
  4. Stir in tomato paste, canned tomatoes and chicken stock
  5. Add in wine, herbs and Worcestershire
  6. Season with salt & pepper
  7. Cover and cook gently 20 minutes
  8. Melt butter in saucepan
  9. Stir in flour and cook for 2 minutes over gentle heat
  10. Remove from heat, stir in milk
  11. Return to heat, stir until thick
  12. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
  13. Add cheese, stirring until cheese melts
  14. Preheat oven to 180Â°C
  15. Pour half of meat sauce into lightly greased pan
  16. Alternate layers of lasagne, meat and white sauce
  17. End with white sauce
  18. Sprinkle with cheddar or Parmesan
  19. Cook 35-40 minutes
  20. Stand 5 minutes before serving
